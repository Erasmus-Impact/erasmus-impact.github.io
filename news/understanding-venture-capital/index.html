New Workshop: Understanding Venture Capital

New Workshop: Understanding Venture Capital

  • May 13, 2021
  • Posted by Moritz Bergenthal
  • Announcement, Event

As part of the Erasmus+ Project IMPACT, we are launching a series of talks related to entrepreneurship. Whilst the project is mainly focused on marine robotics and educational transdisciplinary aspects of this field, this specific workshop series is of a more general interest.

The first talk will be on 13th May, 4 pm - 7 pm

Title: Understanding Venture Capital
Speaker: Jakob Gajšek

For more information, visit the project page over at Understanding Venture Capital
