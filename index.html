About Us

Create and develop a sustainable, international, and multi-disciplinary network in the area of intelligent marine systems

With the purpose of: Fostering cooperation in research and learning – Exchanging good practices and innovative approaches – Providing a forum for discussion of relevant ideas and topics – Linking ideas and practises to the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals

We want to: Create novel study material in the field of marine systems – Foster an entrepreneurial mindset and creative thinking – Assist in bridging the gap between academia and industry – Create opportunities for students to participate in Intensive Study Programs
